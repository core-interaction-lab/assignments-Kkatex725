ABOUT

DATABASE CREATOR:       ARJUN
WEBSITE DESIGN:       QIAN

CONCEPT:

The concept of this project is to design an online experience for the collection called Behind the Scene (Evolution of Cinema), which lists 100 images of film makers behind the scene from Metropolis (1927)- The Batman (2022). The 100 unique photos will be classified by publish years in the form of gallery that shows more about the timeline and form up a time travel of historical evolution immersively based on the film listed in the collection. Transition between forground and background is also one point I want to emphasize through this design system.
