SUPPORT SHANGHAI
For this project, I will create an interface design for the material assistance online platform for Shanghai. All of the communities in Shanghai have been blocked due to the COVID-19 pandemic recently, and there are lots of citizens, who are unable to get distributed supplies and assistance from the government on account of the population, fussy procedures, and policies. In this interface, viewers will be able to browse all of the info asking for help, such as daily supplies, aiming to solve this problem by providing a functional and affordable tool.
